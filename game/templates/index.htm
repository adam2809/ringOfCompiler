What is Ring of Compiler?

Ring of Compiler is the ultimate drunk coding challenge (probably the only drunk coding challenge apart from trying to complete your PGA coursework after a heavy night out at Rock City.) the aim of the game is to complete the program and produce the required output in 5:00 minutes and submit it without any errors. But be warned there are alcohol themed consequences for any errors and even greater consequences for not finishing!
